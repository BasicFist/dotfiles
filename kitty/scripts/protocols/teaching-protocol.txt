 Teaching Protocol
═══════════════════════════════════════

Expert Responsibilities:
  • Explain concepts clearly
  • Provide examples and analogies
  • Check for understanding frequently
  • Adapt depth and pace to the learner

Learner Responsibilities:
  • Ask clarifying questions
  • Attempt exercises out loud
  • Summarize takeaways in your own words
  • Share areas that still feel fuzzy

Flow:
  1. Explain the concept
  2. Explore examples together
  3. Practice with an exercise
  4. Check understanding
  5. Mark concepts as mastered

Commands:
  • Explain: ai-teach-explain.sh "<concept>" "<explanation>"
  • Question: ai-teach-question.sh <learner> "<question>"
  • Exercise: ai-teach-exercise.sh "<exercise>" [difficulty]
  • Check: ai-teach-check.sh <learner> "<summary>"
  • Mastered: ai-teach-mastered.sh "<concept>"

Goal:
  • Bring the learner to confident mastery of the topic
