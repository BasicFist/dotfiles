ACTIVATION INSTRUCTIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The new True Neon theme is ready in: ~/.config/kitty/aesthetics.conf

To activate:
  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+F5 in Kitty (reload config)
  2. Or run: kitty @ load-config
  3. Or restart Kitty

To test visual effects:
  - Ctrl+Alt+G  → Pulse effect
  - Ctrl+Alt+F  → Flicker effect
  - Ctrl+Alt+P  → Stop effects

Expected result:
  ⚡ Pure black background
  ⚡ Electric cyan text
  ⚡ Hot pink cursor with long trail
  ⚡ Bright cyan borders (2px thick)
  ⚡ Hot pink active tabs
